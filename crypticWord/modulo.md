## Modulo Range

**Example**
```
Range: 30 - 50
20 digits between

0 to x % x + 1

30 - 30 = 0
50 - 30 = 20

digit Range: 0 - 20

35 - 30 = 5
5 % 21 = 5 remainder
5/20 = 5 with a remainder of 1

55 - 30 = 25
25 % 21 = 4
25/21 = 1 with a remainder of 4

4 + 30 = 34

34 falls in the range of 30 - 50

```
